ABOUT

The main objective of the session is to give the participants a clear-cut idea on Artificial Intelligence in robotics, their features and thier applications. By the end of the session the participants will gain proficient knowlegde abou AI and ML and how to apply them efficiently in various fields.

DETAILS

In this event, the participants will gain knowledge about the basics of Artificial Intelligence in robotics, then they will be explained in detail on the operations involved and the various fields in which AI and ML are implemented. This will be a productive session for the attendeers and will enable them with context regarding the foundation of AI & ML and its vast applications. This session would immensely benefit the participants who wish to seek experience in AI in robotics

Resource Person : Mr. Lakshman Subramanyam, General Manager, Apple Incorp., USA.
