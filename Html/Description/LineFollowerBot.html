LINE FOLLOWER BOT
Register

ABOUT

In this event, the bots fabricated by each team would would be tested for their endurance, durability and their line following accuracy. The bot has to compete in 5 laps following the given line. Awards will be given based on the shortest time interval on which the bot completes every lap.

DETAILS

--> A team can have a maximum of 4 members or individually from B.tech/B.E/Diploma. No participants can be part of more than one team.

--> If a team is paricipating, only the team leader has to register.

--> The bot should be as per the given specification.

--> The bot should not damage the arena.

--> The bot should follow the line which will be made with bends and turns, if failed penalty will be given.
