VIDEO VISIONS

ABOUT

Creativity is the word of action and the output can be a beautiful vision. VideoVisions brings out the hovering creativity in you through video editing skills. The radical message that you tend to convey through your video can be attained by an incredible way of storytelling. Music gives an intense feel to your video to be wholesome. The trending transition editorials that attract this generation, create an exquisite touch to your video. Montage of various videos combined together with a good screenplay, music, and transition. VideoVision lets you encompass all these elements to explore your video editing skills and to develop them.

DETAILS

Round 1

The first round is an acquaintance round. The participants are asked to send a video file that lasts for one minute or under, (only one video, under any one of the topic given). The best of the videos from this round will be qualified for the second round.

Round 2

The participants qualified for the second round “Montage in time” are asked to create a montage video with various transitions required for the given topic. The music used for the videos (for both first and second rounds) can be either a song or BGM. EFX audio can also be included. The best of the videos with on-point transition, BGM, and screenplay under the given topic will be recognized. The participants are asked to send their videos through google forms shared with them via WhatsApp. The video must contain a watermark of the participant’s name and access number given to them. The deadline for submitting the video will be on the next day of the first round. The participants submitting their response before the deadline will be given a participation certificate and the best two will be recognized.

Date & Timings

Round 1 : Submission within 02:00 PM on 11.09.2022

Round 2 : Submission within 11:59 PM on 13.09.2022

Last date for registration is 10.09.2022 within 10:00 PM
