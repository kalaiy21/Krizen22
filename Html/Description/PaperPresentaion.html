ABOUT

A contest that arouse you with emerging ideas which makes revolutionary changes in this universe. The main aim of this event is to enhance the participant's ability in writing or making papers as well as to present it, and also to broaden the mind of the participants. And to encourage their desire to enhance their knowledge towards technical aspects. A paper presentation is an idea by itself.

DETAILS

Themes

  1. Robotics and Automation
  2. Smart Agriculture
  3. Blockchain
  4. Alternate energy and fuel
  5. Micro factories
  6. IoT home
  7. Smart vehicles
  8. Digital twin

Round 1

The first round will be abstract selection. The participating teams should submit the abstract of their paper one week before the event, via google forms that will be provided. This will be an elimination round, thus the teams will be screened based on the quality and content of their abstract. The best 10 â€“ 15 abstracts will be qualified for the next round. The teams to qualify for the second round will be receiving a participation certificate.

Round 2

In the second round, the selected teams will be invited to the college to present the papers. The presentation should not exceed 5 mins. The papers should also be submitted via Google forms that will be provided at the time of presentation. The judges will analyze the technical content, quality of paper and way of expression. Based on the scores from judges the top two papers will be awarded.

Date & Timings

Round 1 : Submission within 6:00 PM on 10.09.2022

Round 2 : Commences on 15.09.2022 @ 10:00 AM

Last date for registration is 08.09.2022 within 10:00 PM
