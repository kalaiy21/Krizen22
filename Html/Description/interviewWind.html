ABOUT

This interview series helps the participants to improve their self-presentation. It analyses and provide constructive feedback on participant's communication skills. Mock interview helps candidates to get an overview of what you can expect in a real job interview,boost confidence, identify strength and weaknesses, reduce anxiety by familiarizing with the situation. The participants can add the certificate to resume and showcase their skills.

DETAILS

Round 1

The first round is the Aptitude test. Aptitude tests will test your ability to perform tasks and react to situations at work. This round will consist of a set of 30 multiple choice questions with only one correct answer. This round contains questions on - numerical reasoning, logical reasoning, and verbal reasoning. The participants will be screened for the next round based on their scores.

Round 2

In the second round, selected participants will be grouped into teams. Group discussion will be organized. Each team will be provided with an on-spot topic (non-technical). Each team should discuss on the given topic. Group discussion will test their soft skills and their ability to cope up with various situations. Based on the scores given by the judges, participants of the team will be qualified for the next round.

Round 3

The final round is a one-to-one interview. One-to-one interview comprises general questions which focus on behavioral characteristics, problem-solving abilities, and your ability to work in a team as well as independently. Based on the scores given by the judges, the participants will be provided with certificates.
